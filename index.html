What does it mean to Step Into The Sun?

Step into the sun began as a mantra for founder, Taylor, who struggles with bi-polar disorder and severe depression. Step into the sun means stepping into your light. It means becomming your own sunshine. It means finding love and walking towards the light, even when the darkness feels all consuming. It's a reminder that each of us is so much more than our pain, more than our demons, more than our mental illness(es). Your life matters. Your mental health matters. You matter.
