What does it mean to Step Into The Sun?

Step Into The Sun began as a simple mantra for founder, Taylor Dalton, who struggles with bi-polar disorder and severe depression. Step Into The Sun means stepping into your greatness. It means becoming your own sunshine. It means walking towards the light, even when the darkness feels all consuming. It's a reminder that each of us is so much more than our pain, more than our demons, more than our mental illness(es). It's a safe space to grow, to learn, to heal, and to show that no one is truly alone.
Your mental health matters.
Your life matters.
You matter.
