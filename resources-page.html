Additional Resources

Step Into The Sun is not an encyclopedia and we do know have all the answers. We are a place for real people to share real stories and a place for some educational reference. However, it is imperative that if you need medical assistance, please contact a medical professional.

Though Step Into The Sun does everything in its power to stop the spread of misinformation, it's important to do your own research. What works for someone else may not work for you. Someone else's experience may be different from yours. It's important to know yourself first and to do what is best for YOUR personal mental health situation and journey.

Below we have compiled a list of resources, professional and not, to further aid you in your mental health journey. Below you'll find the suicide and crisis lifeline, as well as other blogs, nonprofits, books, and content creators you can check out to further your support for and understanding of mental health. If you have any suggestions for other resources we can add or anything you want to add to the list, head over to our contact page and shoot us a message.
